# PlaceHub (Corner Backend)

PlaceHub is a social map and places sharing application designed to help users discover and share their favorite spots with friends.

## ✅ Completed Features

### 🔐 Authentication & Security

- **Secure Registration**: Create an account with email verification.
- **Login System**: Secure access to your personal account.
- **Data Protection**: Your password and personal data are encrypted and secure.

### 👤 User Profiles

- **Personal Profile**: Customize your profile with a bio, avatar, and contact info.
- **Public Profiles**: View other users' profiles to see who they are.
- **Profile Management**: Easily update your information at any time.

### 👥 Social Network (Follow System)

- **Follow Users**: Connect with friends and interesting people.
- **Unfollow**: Manage your network by unfollowing users.
- **Followers & Following**: See who follows you and who you are following.
- **Real-time Counts**: Instantly see your follower and following stats.
